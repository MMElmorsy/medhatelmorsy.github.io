Research Interests

  • System-Level Uncertainty in Earthquake Engineering
    Benchmarking, model validation, and probabilistic assessment.
  • Additive Manufacturing for Physical Modeling
    3D-printed reinforcement, micro-concrete, binder jetting for masonry, and centrifuge testing.
  • Seismic Performance of RC and URM Structures
    Experimental and numerical studies for resilience-based design.
  • Ground Motion Processing & Rocking Structures
    Filtering effects, dynamic stability, and energy dissipation.
