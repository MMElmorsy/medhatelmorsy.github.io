Medhat Elmorsy

Postdoctoral Researcher · Structural & Earthquake Engineering
Institute of Structural Engineering (IBK), ETH Zurich

I am a postdoctoral researcher at ETH Zurich working at the intersection of earthquake engineering, structural dynamics, and digital construction. My work focuses on system-level uncertainty, additive manufacturing for physical modeling, and the development of benchmark datasets for seismic model validation. I am the recipient of the Swiss National Science Foundation Postdoc.Mobility Grant (CHF 186,666) and will conduct my postdoctoral research at MIT.

Research Interests

  • Earthquake engineering and seismic risk
  • System-level uncertainty and model validation
  • Structural dynamics and nonlinear response
  • Additive manufacturing for physical modeling
  • Performance-based and resilience-based design
  • Experimental and numerical analysis of RC and URM structures
